About netedu

Netedu is a young company with some history in Puerto Rico. It was founded and is led by Antonio Vantaggiato, the open education enthusiast who brought Open Source and Moodle to Puerto Rico.

At netedu, we do education! Web-based, open, working educational solutions and experiences for students, faculty, admin and institutions.

Netedu loves to do Web portals, Ed-tech projects, faculty development, competency-based Web 2.0 course design... or redesign.

Ed Tech

Netedu loves Ed-tech! We enjoy implementing beautiful, high-impact open ed infrastructures and experiences.

Domains of One's Own, Student (and Faculty) Web Publishing and Portfolios, Open (online or distance) courses are opur specialty.
